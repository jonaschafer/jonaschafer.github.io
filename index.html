Jon Schafer is a designer, art director and illustrator living in Portland, Oregon. He's currently leading brand design at Clockwise.

Previously he's worked on nearly every type of design project, including logos, illustrations, reels, shirts, fonts, interiors and so many websites.

Most days you can find him running around a nearby dormant volcano, frequenting Forest Park with his family, and occasionally emailing.

Sometimes he talks in the third person.
