Jon Schafer is a designer, art director and illustrator working under the moniker Why, Hello There. He's currently working as a Product Designer at Zapier.

In the past he's worked on nearly every type of design project, including logos, illustrations, reels, shirts, fonts, interiors and so many websites.

Most days you can find him working near a dormant volcano, frequenting Forest Park with his family, and occasionally emailing.

Sometimes he talks in the third person.
