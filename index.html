Jon Schafer is a designer, art director and illustrator working under the moniker Why, Hello There. He's currently working as a Product Designer at Zapier.

Most days you can find him working near a dormant volcano, and frequenting Forest Park with his family as much as possible.

In the past he's worked on nearly type of design project, including logos, illustrations, reels, shirts, fonts, interiors and a bunch of websites.

Sometimes he talks in the third person.
