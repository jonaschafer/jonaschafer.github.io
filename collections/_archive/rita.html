Rita Allen Foundation

The Rita Allen Foundation is a New Jersey nonprofit focused on innovative approaches, young, promising scholars, and breakthrough solutions in the fields of science, and civic engagement. This modern, responsive website redesign updates Rita Allen's visuals to reflect their mission and character, while adding a powerful resource library for scientists, scholars, and other nonprofits.
