While working at Zapier, we used Jekyll (a static site generator) on an internal branding project. It wasn't my first experience with code, but it was my first time working with Jekyll. I was struck by how refreshingly straightforward the setup was, and resolved to start using it weekly to encourage myself to code, and write, more.

So this is a journal of sorts, dedicated to capturing the observations of my two kids, Levi and Cassidy. The moments that photos and videos can't capture, and are sometimes the hardest to remember.
