While working at Zapier, we used Jekyll (a static site generator) on an internal branding project. It wasn't my first experience with code, but it was my first time working with Jekyll. I was struck by how refreshingly straightforward the setup was, and resolved to start using it weekly to encourage myself to code, and in a roundabout way, to write more.

So, this is a collection of articles dedicated to capturing the observations and feeling I have around my two kids, Levi and Cassidy. Those moments that photos and videos can't capture, and are sometimes the hardest to remember.
